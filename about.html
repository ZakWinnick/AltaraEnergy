Where Charging Becomes Part of Your Journey

America's first premium EV charging network for scenic highway corridors

Altara Energy is America's first premium EV charging network designed for scenic highway corridors and National Parks routes. We treat charging as hospitality, not utility, guaranteeing indoor climate-controlled comfort at every location. This is more than infrastructure. This is about making the journey unforgettable.

Indoor Comfort, Every Location

From the Pacific Coast to the Desert Southwest, every Altara location features climate-controlled lounges with premium restrooms, WiFi, and comfortable seating. Whether it's foggy, windy, or blazing hot outside, you'll charge in comfort. Industry first.

Hub-Satellite Operations

Basecamp hubs (6-8 DCFC, café, regional NOC) support Waystations (4 DCFC, lounge, automated retail) for 99% uptime and approximately 2-hour response times. Regional operations ensure you're never stranded.

Built for Adventure

EV road-trippers exploring scenic corridors, adventure travelers visiting National Parks, and locals who value predictable, premium charging experiences. We serve those who see driving as part of the destination.

Built for the Journey, Not Just the Charge

While urban networks compete on convenience and highway networks chase ubiquity, Altara focuses on the underserved premium scenic corridor market. We're building where the drive is part of the destination: Route 66, Pacific Coast Highway, roads to National Parks, and America's most beautiful byways.

Our category-defining advantage is straightforward: guaranteed indoor comfort. While competitors leave drivers exposed to weather in parking lots, we provide climate-controlled lounges, clean restrooms, and premium amenities at every location. Whether you're at a staffed Basecamp with a full café or an automated Waystation with micro-market, you'll always charge indoors.

We're not infrastructure providers. We're hospitality operators who happen to provide charging. We're journey enablers, transforming necessary stops into memorable moments along America's most iconic routes.
