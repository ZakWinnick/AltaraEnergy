Baker Basecamp

Baker, California - Historic Route 66

Gateway to Mojave National Preserve and the historic heart of Route 66, Baker Basecamp will be our flagship location, setting the standard for premium scenic corridor charging. This full-service destination will feature comprehensive amenities, a regional operations center, and serve as the hub for satellite Waystations throughout the Mojave Desert region.

Waystation Network - Supporting the Hub

Baker Basecamp will anchor a network of automated Waystations throughout the Mojave region, providing reliable charging at strategic intervals with 24/7 monitoring and rapid response support.

Mojave Desert Corridors

Strategic Waystations supporting I-15, I-40, and routes to Death Valley and Mojave National Preserve. Climate-controlled comfort in the desert heat.
