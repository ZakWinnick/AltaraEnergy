Common Questions

Is membership really free?

Yes. Altara Network membership is completely free with no monthly fees. You only pay for the electricity you use at our flat rate of $0.47/kWh.

What's the difference between member and non-member pricing?

Non-members pay time-of-use rates that vary throughout the day, with higher costs during peak hours. Members always pay the same flat rate of $0.47/kWh, no matter when they charge.

Do I need to use the app to charge?

Members can use the app for the easiest experience - it shows real-time availability, lets you start sessions with your phone, and tracks your usage history. Non-members can also charge using a credit card at the charger.

Is this network good for road trips?

Altara Network is designed primarily for daily drivers and commuters who need reliable, predictable charging for their regular routes. Our network focuses on convenience and consistency for everyday use.

When can I start using Altara Network?
